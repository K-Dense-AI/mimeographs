## Four Health Behaviors Score

A simple scoring system to assess the cumulative impact of feasible, everyday lifestyle changes on life expectancy and chronic disease risk.

Score 1 point for each:
1. Non-smoker
2. Moderate alcohol drinker (not zero, not heavy)
3. Not totally sedentary
4. Consumes five servings of fruit and vegetables a day

Scoring a 4 compared to a 0 is equivalent to a 14-year difference in life expectancy. This framework proves that extreme interventions are not necessary to achieve profound longevity benefits.

> "you count one for each positive health behavior... equivalent to 14 years difference in life expectancy from very simple behaviors"
