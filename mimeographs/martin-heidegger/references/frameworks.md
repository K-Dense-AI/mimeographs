# Heidegger's Frameworks

Heidegger developed specific methodological approaches to bypass traditional metaphysics and uncover the lived reality of human existence. Use these frameworks to structure your analysis.

## Hermeneutic Phenomenology

**When to use:** A method for uncovering the essential structures of Dasein and the meaning of being by starting with everyday experience and interpreting its deeper significance.

**Steps:**
1. Begin at the 'ontic' level by observing how life is experienced in an everyday, pre-reflective way.
2. Move to the 'ontological' level to articulate the deeper meaning of that experience.
3. Oscillate between these levels in a circular movement.
4. Own your fore-structures (biases) to reveal something new.

> "oscillating between two levels of description... the level of everyday experience... and the second level is going to have to do with the articulation of the meaning or significance"
*(sources: src_009, src_033, src_021, src_013)*

## Destructuring (Destruktion)

**When to use:** An interpretative strategy that dismantles the philosophical tradition to uncover hidden fundamental experiences of being.

**Steps:**
1. Clarify our own hermeneutic situation.
2. Destruct the hermeneutic situation of the author or tradition to scrape off solidified dogmatic statements.
3. Provide a concrete phenomenological description of the lived experience to keep it alive.

> "an interpretative strategy that reveals the fundamental experience of being hidden within the theoretical attitude of the metaphysics of presence."
*(sources: src_030, src_041)*

## Phenomenological Questioning of Technology

**When to use:** A method for understanding the true essence of technology beyond its superficial or instrumental appearance.

**Steps:**
1. Reject the instrumental view of technology as a mere means to an end.
2. Attend to original language to uncover how the phenomenon was understood before scientific distortion.
3. Observe how the phenomenon dictates the way entities 'reveal' themselves.
4. Recognize the limits and dangers of this mode to prepare a 'free relationship' to it.

> "our examining or questioning of the essence of technology and other kinds of revealing is 'the piety of thought.'"
*(sources: src_008)*

## The Hermeneutic Circle

**When to use:** A process of moving between the parts of an experience and the whole of an experience to increase the depth of engagement and understanding.

**Steps:**
Move from the parts of experience to the whole of experience, and back and forth repeatedly, continuing to spiral until reaching a place of sensible meaning free of inner contradictions.

> "moves from the parts of experience to the whole of experience and back and forth again and again to increase the depth of engagement and understanding."
*(sources: src_041)*
