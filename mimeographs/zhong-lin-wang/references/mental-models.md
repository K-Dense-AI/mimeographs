## High Entropy Energy
Viewing ambient mechanical energy as a vast, highly disordered, and low-quality energy source that requires a fundamentally different harvesting paradigm.

**Rationale:** Instead of relying on concentrated, high-quality fossil fuels or high-speed turbines, this model focuses on capturing the ubiquitous, irregular, and low-frequency movements of daily life (human motion, wind, raindrops, ocean waves).

## Displacement Current Lens
Understanding power generation through time-varying electric fields rather than just moving charges in a wire.

**Rationale:** Traditional generators use conduction current (moving charges driven by Lorentz force). TENGs use Maxwell's displacement current (a time-varying electric field created by the physical separation of triboelectric charges), allowing for efficient power generation without magnets.

> "The story of nanogenerators starts from an additional term, now known as Wang term, in Maxwell's equations, due to the non-electric field-induced and strain-related polarization..."
*(sources: src_011, src_012, src_026)*

## Electron Cloud Overlap Model
Reasoning about triboelectricity as a quantum mechanical process rather than macro-scale friction.

**Rationale:** When two materials are pressed together, their inter-atomic distance becomes shorter than the normal bonding length. This lowers the potential barrier and forces electron wave functions to overlap, facilitating electron transfer.

> "So when your two atoms become so close, the barrier between the two will be reduced. So you have a strong overlap of electron clouds. As a result, you can have a charge transfer from one atom to the next one."
*(sources: src_010, src_013, src_029)*

## Blue Energy
A paradigm for ocean wave energy harvesting that uses distributed networks of floating TENGs.

**Rationale:** Distinguished from traditional 'green energy', blue energy captures low-frequency, multi-directional wave motion using arrays of floating devices that can wobble in any direction, rather than relying on heavy, unidirectional electromagnetic generators.

> "we have enough blue a green energy we're tired of pre energy right we want the blue blue water so blue energy"
*(sources: src_010, src_011)*

## Strain as a Gate Voltage (Piezotronics)
Viewing mechanical strain as an internal electrical gate voltage that controls electron transport.

**Rationale:** Rather than seeing strain merely as physical deformation, piezotronics uses the resulting piezoelectric potential to control the flow of electrons in a circuit, enabling force- or pressure-controlled electronics.

> "if you apply a string this voltage control the amount of electron you can transport so therefore we can build a transistor use a strain or force or pressure controll Electronics"
*(sources: src_027)*

## The Tunable Barrier
Conceptualizing the Schottky barrier at a metal-semiconductor interface as a dynamically adjustable wall.

**Rationale:** Photons (lasers) lower the wall to increase conductivity, while piezoelectric strain raises the wall to restrict it. Balancing these two forces allows for precise optimization of optoelectronic devices.
