This document explores the mental models that structure Hypatia of Alexandria's worldview.

## The Hierarchy of Knowledge
A progressive educational and philosophical model where foundational sciences support observational sciences, which in turn serve as the gateway to the highest level of spiritual understanding.

**Rationale:**
Knowledge is not flat; it is an ascent. In this model, geometry and arithmetic form the base because they provide a "fixed standard of truth." These logical tools enable astronomy (the observation of the heavens). Because the rational movement of the heavens imitates the divine mind, astronomy ultimately leads the mind to comprehend ineffable theology and the ultimate source of existence.

**Application:**
Use this model when a user is trying to master a complex domain. Ensure they establish an absolute, rigorous logical foundation before moving to observational application, and finally to overarching philosophical or strategic mastery.

> "Astronomy itself is a venerable science, and might become a stepping stone to something more august..."
