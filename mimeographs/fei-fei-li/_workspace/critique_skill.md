# Critique of SKILL.md

**Score:** 7/10

The artifact successfully captures Fei-Fei Li's 'pragmatic optimism' and her pivot toward spatial intelligence, avoiding generic AI platitudes. However, it completely drops her strong advocacy for diversity in tech and the democratization of AI resources, which are prominent in the corpus. Fixing this coverage gap and tightening some repetitive principles will make this an excellent, highly accurate skill.

## Strengths

- Excellent use of her specific idioms, particularly 'Wordsmiths in the Dark' and 'Digital Cambrian Explosion'.
- Accurately frames her stance on AGI and LLMs without falling into generic doomerism or utopianism.
- The 'Virtuous Cycle of Spatial Intelligence' framework is well-synthesized, highly actionable, and perfectly grounded in the corpus.

## Issues

### High

- **[coverage]** _SKILL.md > description & references/principles.md_
  - The artifact lists 'diversity in tech' as a trigger in the main description, but completely omits the 'Diverse Voices in AI' principle and 'Homogenous AI Development' anti-pattern found in the corpus. This leaves the trigger unsupported.
  - _Suggestion:_ Add 'Diverse Voices in AI' to the Core Principles and 'Homogenous AI Development' to the Anti-patterns to accurately reflect her advocacy.

### Medium

- **[coverage]** _references/principles.md_
  - The corpus heavily emphasizes 'Democratize AI Resources and Public Sector Evaluation' as a necessary counterbalance to industry monopolies. This governance stance is entirely missing from the artifact.
  - _Suggestion:_ Include the democratization of AI resources as a core principle to round out her views on AI governance.
- **[coverage]** _references/anti-patterns.md_
  - The artifact misses the 'Fixating on AGI' anti-pattern. The corpus explicitly notes her critique of treating AGI as a marketing buzzword rather than a rigorous scientific goal, which is a crucial nuance to her worldview.
  - _Suggestion:_ Add 'Fixating on AGI' to the anti-patterns list.

### Low

- **[duplication]** _references/heuristics.md_
  - The heuristic 'Demand AI That Can Do' is nearly identical in phrasing and meaning to the principle 'Perception is for Action' and the anti-pattern 'Stopping at Passive Perception'.
  - _Suggestion:_ Replace this heuristic with 'Horizon Goal Resilience' from the corpus to add variety and cover her advice on pushing through institutional friction.
- **[structure]** _SKILL.md > How Fei-Fei Li reasons_
  - The main file points to `references/mental-models.md` at the end of the reasoning section, but unlike Frameworks and Principles, there is no dedicated 'Mental Models' section summarizing the top 2-3 models in the main file.
  - _Suggestion:_ Add a brief 'Mental Models' section in the main file explicitly highlighting 'The Digital Cambrian Explosion' and 'Wordsmiths in the Dark'.
