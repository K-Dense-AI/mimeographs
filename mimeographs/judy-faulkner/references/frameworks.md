This document details the structured frameworks Judy Faulkner uses to manage customer relationships, software design, and long-term corporate governance.

## Mitigating EHR-Driven Physician Burnout
A three-pronged approach to improving physician satisfaction and reducing friction with electronic health records.

1. **Personalization**: Allow every physician to adjust settings so the software matches their specific workflow.
2. **Continuous Training**: Provide excellent initial training and ongoing 'at the elbow' support to uncover unknown capabilities.
3. **Zero Committees**: Utilize 'physician builders' and reduce the number of committees required for a technical change to zero.

> "KLAS found three things were most important for Epic customers: personalization for physicians... initial training... how many committees does it go through? The best answer is zero."
(sources: src_010, src_020)

## Customer Empathy & BFF Alignment
A framework for ensuring continuous, deep understanding of a specific client's high-stakes needs.

1. Send programmers on annual immersive visits to customer sites (e.g., the Emergency Department) to observe end-users.
2. Assign a specific staff member to a customer as their 'Best Friend Forever' (BFF).
3. Make the BFF responsible for deeply learning the client's best practices to share globally, while bringing external best practices back to the client.

> "And you feel this enormous sense of responsibility to do right by them."
(sources: src_010, src_034)

## Trust-Based Succession Governance
A legal and structural framework to ensure a company remains private and mission-driven after the founder's death.

1. Transfer all voting shares into a dedicated trust upon death.
2. Establish a voting committee of trusted family members and long-time employees to govern the trust.
3. Set strict, unbreakable rules (e.g., the company cannot go public or be acquired).
4. Create an independent oversight board of customers whose sole job is to sue the voting committee if they violate the rules.

(sources: src_006)
