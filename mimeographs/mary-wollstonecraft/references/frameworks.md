## First Principles Questioning
A method for dismantling societal prejudices and customs by returning to fundamental axioms of human nature.

When societal prejudices, customs, or arguments of "expediency" have clouded reason, apply these steps:
1. Strip away prevailing prejudices and artificial manners.
2. Ask plain, axiomatic questions about fundamental human nature (e.g., what separates humans from animals).
3. Set these simple axioms alongside the deviations that current circumstances and customs have brought.
4. Build your arguments on these foundational truths rather than accepting what is merely "expedient".

> "In the present state of society it seems that we have to go back to first principles in search of the simplest truths, and to fight against some prevailing prejudice for every inch of ground."
